About

Hello, I am Bryce

  • 2010-2013

    College Life in Mississippi

    2010 I moved Clinton, MS in order to go to Mississippi College: A Christian University and graduated in 2013 with a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration and majored in accounting.

  • 2014-2016

    Travel Life

    After college, I decided to take opportunities to travel and live in various places. Ended up living in Norfolk, VA and Springfield, MO. I visited many places within the US such as Boston, New York City, Savannah, GA, and Key West, Fl to name a few. Outside the US, I visited the Bahamas and Panama.

  • 2016-2017

    Florida Life

    In 2016, I got the opportunity to move to Clearwater, Florida and became a certified Real Estate agent and worked on a team of Realtors. During that time, I became the photographer of our listings. Along with created websites with various online tools such as Wix, and Wordpress to create websites and felt drawn to doing web development, but felt as though I was too old to start.

  • 2018-2019

    Becoming a web developer

    In the beginning of 2018, I got a job working as a long-term substitute teacher and was offered the full-time teaching role for the next year. During the same time, I was in contact with a company called Quantrex Research, Inc and was offered a chance to come and learn web development. I decided to not take the contract and left to persue becoming a web developer. After working there for about 10 months I decided to increase my learning pace by going to Dev Mountain Web Development Bootcamp.
